Hi! I'm Saumya (pronounced sawm-yaa), and I'm an artist and designer based in Maharashtra, India. Currently, I'm pursuing my B.Des+M.Des (a 5-year dual degree in design) from the IDC School of Design, IIT Bombay, and am in the process of taking my first steps into the world of animation.

As an artist, I adore participating in visual development, toying with color, and training myself to tell great stories. I always love a good challenge, or a tricky puzzle. I'm also a drummer, a bharatnatyam dancer, and an avid seafood-enjoyer.
