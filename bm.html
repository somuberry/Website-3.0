BhoothMaat Inc.

← Home

BhoothMaat Inc. is an interactive comic book, coded in HTML+CSS+JS, about a young man who runs a paranormal helpline in modern-day India. You play as this young man, and make his choices for him during a client visit. Your choices will lead to one of many endings - good, bad, or middling.
